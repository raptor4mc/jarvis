Artificial intelligence (AI) is the field of building computer systems that can perform tasks associated with human intelligence.
Machine learning is a subfield of AI in which models learn patterns from data instead of being explicitly programmed with every rule.
The Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to support life.
The Pacific Ocean is the largest and deepest ocean on Earth, covering more than 30 percent of the planet's surface.
The Internet is a global system of interconnected computer networks that communicate using standardized protocols.
